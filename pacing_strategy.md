## ⚠️ Decision Tree

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Morning RHR ≥ 65 / sick | **Consider DNS** |
| Morning RHR 60-64 | Switch to Safe (8:15/km, walking OK) |
| Rain forecast | -10s/km possible. Watch slippery |
| Temp >33°C at start | Abandon time goal. Hydrate hard. Walk OK |
| Cramp mid-race | Walk 30s + electrolyte. Resume slow |
| Stomach upset | Stop gels. Water only. -30s/km |
| Feel amazing km 10 | **Stick to plan!** Don't accelerate yet |
| Feel terrible km 15 | Walk 1 min at next aid, restart +30s/km |
| **Walking urge km 8-12** | 🚫 **RESIST! Discipline pays off most here** |
